Professional Biography

Engineering reliable, secure networks

I'm Kashaf Ali, a Network Engineer with over 3 years of experience in enterprise networking, IT infrastructure, network security, firewall administration, routing & switching, incident troubleshooting, and enterprise support. I enjoy turning complex infrastructure challenges into stable, secure, well-documented systems.

My day-to-day blends hands-on engineering with a security-first mindset — configuring Cisco switching and VLANs, administering Palo Alto firewalls, managing NAT and ACL policies, and keeping enterprise and medical infrastructure online with high uptime. I've also trained dozens of learners in networking and security fundamentals, which sharpened my ability to communicate technical concepts clearly.

I'm now focused on growing into Network Security and SOC Analyst roles, combining my networking foundation with monitoring, incident response, and threat detection.
