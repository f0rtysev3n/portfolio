Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the Kashaf Ali portfolio website ("this site") handles information. This is a personal portfolio hosted as a static website and is committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

This site does not use tracking cookies or analytics by default. If you submit the contact form, any information you provide (such as your name, email address and message) is used solely to respond to your inquiry. Because the site is static, form submissions are only processed if a third-party form service has been configured by the site owner.

How Information Is Used

  • To respond to messages and inquiries you send.
  • To communicate about potential opportunities or collaborations.

Third-Party Services

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts and icons from a public CDN (Font Awesome). These providers may receive standard request data (such as your IP address) as part of delivering their resources. If a form service is configured, its own privacy policy will apply to submitted data.

Data Retention

Any message you send is retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry, after which it may be deleted.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have shared by contacting the site owner via the details on the Contact page.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please reach out through the Contact page.
